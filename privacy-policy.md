# Privacy Policy for Timers & Pomodoro

_Last updated: 2026-05-01_

Timers & Pomodoro is a standalone Wear OS app developed by Jakob Zech.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal
information.

## What stays on your watch

All app data is stored locally on your watch:

- Your saved timers (names, durations, target times).
- Your saved pomodoro presets (names, concentration / break durations,
  cycle limits, target times, advance mode).
- The current run state of an active timer or pomodoro.
- Today's accumulated focus minutes (resets at midnight, watch-local).
- Your vibration / haptic preferences and the watchface complication's
  default-display preference.

This data is never sent off the watch. There is no cloud sync, no
analytics, no advertising, and no network access.

## Permissions

- **Vibrate** — for the phase-end / timer-expiry haptic.
- **Exact alarm** and **schedule exact alarm** — so the alarm fires at
  the right moment even when the watch is in doze.
- **Wake lock** and **use full-screen intent** — so the alarm wakes the
  display when a timer / pomodoro phase ends.
- **Post notifications** — for the "timer done" notification in the
  notification shade.
- **Receive boot completed** — so a running timer or pomodoro survives a
  watch reboot.

## Children

The app is suitable for general audiences and contains no content
directed at children under 13.
